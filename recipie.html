Shravans Recipie for the Best cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granuated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup drak brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yest,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add in the flour and mix untill combines into a dough.
  3. Add in the flour and mix.
  4. Knead the dcough for 10 minutes
  5. Transpher the dough into large nowl and cover with wrap.
  6. Make cinnomons and ovanize it and enjoy.
